Project Euler No. 1: Multiples of 3 and 5

If we list all the natural numbers below 10 that are multiples of 3 or 5, we get 3, 5, 6 and 9. The sum of these multiples is 23.

Click on the 'Calculate' button to find the sum of all the multiples of 3 or 5 below 1000, as well as a list of all the multiples.
